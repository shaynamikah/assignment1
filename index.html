Early Life and Career

Born in London and raised in Ibiza, Charlotte Tilbury developed a passion for beauty at an early age. She studied at the Glauca Rossi School of Makeup and worked with top fashion designers and celebrities before launching her own brand.

Rise to Fame

Charlotte became one of the most sought-after makeup artists, working with A-list celebrities and major fashion shows. Her signature look, the glowing and flawless "Tilbury Look," became iconic in the industry.

Charlotte Tilbury once said, Give a woman the right makeup, and she can conquer the world.
